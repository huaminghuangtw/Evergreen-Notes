When making plans, think big. When making progress, think small.

---

# [The Sprint vs Marathon Mindset](https://www.google.com/search?q=sprint+vs+marathon+mindset)

* On a **micro**, day-to-day level, approach your work like you’re in a **sprint** — focused, fast, and intense.
* On a **macro**, year-to-year scale, pace yourself like you’re running a **marathon** — steady, strategic, and sustainable.
