生酮飲食 (Ketogenic Diet) — 顯著降低碳水化合物攝取、以脂肪為主要能量來源的飲食模式。最初於 1920 年代，本來是兒童癲癇的治療方案，後來才被廣泛應用於減重和代謝健康改善。

酮體 (Ketone Bodies) — 肝臟在脂肪酸分解過程中生成的有機化合物，可作為替代能源

酮症 (Ketosis) — 身體轉而以脂肪作為主要能源的代謝狀態

生酮作用 (Ketogenesis) — 指脂肪酸分解生成酮體的生化過程

# How Ketosis Works

When carbohydrate intake drops below a certain threshold (~20–50g net carbs/day), the body depletes its glycogen stores and shifts into a fat-burning metabolic state. The liver converts stored body fat and dietary fat into ketone bodies (acetoacetate, β-hydroxybutyrate, and acetone), which can cross the blood-brain barrier as an alternative fuel source for the brain.

This metabolic flexibility — the ability to switch between glucose and ketones — is the underlying mechanism. The transition typically takes 2–7 days, a period often referred to as the “keto adaptation” phase.

Related: [fasting](fasting.md) induces ketosis through a similar mechanism of carbohydrate restriction.

# The Keto Flu & Electrolytes

During the initial transition, many people experience “keto flu” — a cluster of symptoms including fatigue, headache, brain fog, irritability, and muscle cramps. The primary cause is **electrolyte depletion**: as the body flushes out glycogen, it also excretes bound water and electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium).

**Key countermeasure:** Take extra _[salt](salt.md)_ (sodium). Unlike a standard diet where salt restriction is advised, the ketogenic diet significantly increases sodium requirements.(salt.md).

* 補充足夠的電解質（鈉、鉀、鎂）是預防「酮流感」最有效的方法
* Consider magnesium and potassium supplementation if symptoms persist

# MCT Oil

* 是一種分餾椰子油。MCT stands for “Medium Chain Triglycerides (中鏈脂肪酸)” — fatty acids with a shorter molecular structure that allows for more rapid digestion and direct absorption via the portal vein to the liver.
* Unlike LCTs (long-chain triglycerides), MCTs bypass the lymphatic system and are rapidly converted into ketones, making them a powerful tool for elevating blood ketone levels quickly.
* Provides several cognitive and metabolic benefits:
	* Better mental clarity and focus
	* Enhanced satiety
	* Improved insulin sensitivity
	* Increased ketone production
	* Reduced risk of heart disease
* Is better absorbed after being emulsified (乳化)

# 防彈咖啡 (Bulletproof Coffee)

A popular breakfast replacement / fasting-friendly beverage in the keto community.

* **黑咖啡** — provides caffeine and antioxidants
* **無鹽草飼奶油** (unsalted grass-fed butter) — rich in butyrate and CLA (conjugated linoleic acid)
* **椰子油 or MCT 油** — adds ketone-boosting medium-chain triglycerides
