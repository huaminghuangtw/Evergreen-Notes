> “If the only tool you have is a hammer, it is tempting to treat everything as if it were a nail.” — Abraham Maslow

---

= Law of the Instrument

is a cognitive bias that involves an over-reliance on a familiar tool.

When you become comfortable with a certain tool, you start applying it everywhere, even when it doesn’t fit.
