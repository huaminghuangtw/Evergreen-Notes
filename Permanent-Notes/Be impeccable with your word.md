> _“Words are but pictures of our thoughts.” — John Dryden_

> _“Understanding is the heartwood of well-spoken words.” — The Buddha_

---

“Raise your words, not voice. It is rain that grows flowers, not thunder.”

Always be aware of how you are using the word. Avoid using the word to [speak against yourself](Be%20careful%20how%20you%20are%20talking%20to%20yourself.md) or to [Never gossip about others](Never%20gossip%20about%20others.md) about others.
