# Step 1: Self-Assessment

Begin by rating yourself out of 10 in each of the following areas:

* 🏋️‍♂️ Physical Health: Consider your fitness, energy levels, nutrition, and overall physical well-being.
* 🧠 Mental Health: Reflect on your emotional resilience, stress management, and sense of inner peace.
* 👪 Family: Evaluate the quality of your relationships and connection with family members.
* 👯 Friends: Think about your social life, support network, and friendships.
* 💑 Romantic Relationship: Assess the health and satisfaction of your romantic partnership (if applicable).
* 💼 Career: Consider your professional growth, fulfillment, and work-life balance.
* 💰 Finance: Reflect on your financial stability, habits, and progress toward financial goals.
* 📚 Learning: Evaluate your commitment to personal growth, skill development, and curiosity.
* 🌍 Experiences: Think about the richness of your life experiences, adventures, and memories created.

# Step 2: Reflection

Ask yourself:

* Which area am I feeling satisfied with? Why?
* Which area am I feeling dissatisfied with? What factors contribute to this feeling?

Take time to [journal](journaling.md) your thoughts. Be honest and [compassionate with yourself](self-compassion.md).

# Step 3: Visioning the Future

Now, imagine you’re having a celebratory dinner with a friend in a year’s time. For each area, describe what a 10 out of 10 would look like. Be as specific and vivid as possible:

* What habits, achievements, or changes would make you feel proud?
* What challenges did you overcome?
* How do you feel in each area?

This vision serves as a guide for setting actionable goals and tracking progress throughout the year.
