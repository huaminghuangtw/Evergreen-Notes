= 抗性澱粉

---

are starch molecules (or carbohydrates) that resist digestion, functioning like fiber. They do not break down into sugar and are not absorbed by the small intestine.

# Examples

1. Beans, Peas, and Lentils
2. Overnight whole grains including oats and barley
3. ⭐️ Cooled, cooked rice or potatoes
