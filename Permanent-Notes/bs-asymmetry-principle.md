---

aka “Brandolini’s Law” (formulated by Italian programmer [Alberto Brandolini](https://www.google.com/search?q=Alberto+Brandolini) in 2013)

---

The amount of energy needed to _refute_ bullshit is an order of magnitude bigger than that needed to _produce_ it.

This principle highlights how misinformation, false/fake claims, or “BS” can be created quickly and spread easily, but debunking or correcting it requires much more effort, time, and resources.

---

Reality is complicated.

Correcting falsehoods is labor-intensive and slow.

It is easier to relate a simple lie than a complex truth.

---

1. Media literacy and fact-checking.
