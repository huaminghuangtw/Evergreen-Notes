Design thinking is a human-centered approach to [innovation](Innovation.md) and [problem-solving](Problem%20Solving.md) that draws from the designer’s toolkit to integrate the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

# The Process

1. **Empathize:** Observe, engage, and immerse yourself in users’ experiences.
2. **Define:** Synthesize findings into clear problem statements.
3. **Ideate:** Brainstorm and challenge assumptions to find innovative solutions.
4. **Prototype:** Create low-fidelity versions to explore potential solutions.
5. **Test:** Refine solutions through user feedback and iteration.

# Synthesize (合成) + Synergize (協同)

* **Synthesize:** Combine diverse research findings and insights to form a holistic understanding of the problem space.
* **Synergize:** Collaborate across disciplines, leveraging collective creativity to generate solutions that are greater than the sum of their parts.
