---

Adaptability (& Flexibility) is the single most powerful trait in life—the ability and willingness to pivot quickly and follow new information or evidence.

---

The highest intelligence isn’t knowing—it’s adapting. Knowledge matters, but the ability to learn, unlearn, and change course/direction matters more. <mark>The rigid hold on to what was; the adaptable move with what is and flow toward what will be.</mark>

---

You’ll only go as far as your ability to absorb life’s punches and pivot (軸轉) on the fly.

---

[If you do not bend, you will break.](https://jamesclear.com/3-2-1/february-13-2025)

> “There is nothing permanent except change.” — Heraclitus

> “Nothing endures but change.” — Heraclitus

Fear uncertainty and you fear life. To fear the unknown is to fear life itself. Life without unpredictability isn’t life, not for human beings.
