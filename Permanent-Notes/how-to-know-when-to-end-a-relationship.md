# Good questions to ask yourself

* If someone told me that I am a lot like my partner, would this be a compliment to me?
* Would I want my child/future child to date someone like my partner?
* Is this relationship making me fulfilled or just less lonely?
* Am I able to be unapologetically myself or do I feel the need to show up differently to please my partner?
* Am I in love with my partner as a whole or am I only in love with their good side, their potential or the idea of them?
* Is this how I want to feel like for the rest of my life?
* Is this really how I deserve to be treated?
* Am I genuinely enjoying his/her company?
