= 燕麥甜餅 ≈ Pocket Porridge ≈ Granola [^1]/Oat Bar

# Brief History

Flapjacks are a classic British treat, popular since the 17th century. They are a staple in UK lunchboxes and cafés.

# Key Ingredients

* Brown sugar
* Golden syrup
* [Oat](Oat.md)
* Salt

# Variations & Add-ins

- Dried fruits (raisins, cranberries)
- Nuts or seeds
- Chocolate chips
- Coconut flakes

# Recipe

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a baking tin with parchment paper. 
2. Mix the ingredients together, press into the tin, and bake for about 15-
20 minutes for softer and chewy flavours. Add 5 more minutes for crunch flavours
3. Leave in the tin until almost cold, then cut into bars/pieces.

# Storage Tips

Store in an airtight container for up to a week. Flapjacks freeze well for longer storage.
