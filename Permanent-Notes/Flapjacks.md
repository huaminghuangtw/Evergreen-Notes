= 燕麥甜餅 ≈ Pocket Porridge ≈ Granola [^1]/Oat Bar

# Key Ingredients

* Brown sugar
* Golden syrup
* [Oat](Oat.md)
* Salt

20 minutes for softer and chewy flavours. Add 5 more minutes for crunch flavours.

Leave in the tin until almost cold, then cut into pieces.

[^1]: 由烤穀粒、堅果和水果乾製成的麥片
