Adenosine (腺苷) is a neurotransmitter that promotes a person's need to sleep.

* The build-up of adenosine accumulates the longer we're awake during the day
* An increase in adenosine induces a sense of sleepiness/drowsiness (sleep drive/pressure)

When you sleep: Adenosine → APT (“energy currency”)

When you are awake: APT → Adenosine
