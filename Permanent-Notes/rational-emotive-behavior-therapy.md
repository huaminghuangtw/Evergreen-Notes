# The ABC Model

1. **A (Activating Event):** The external event.
2. **B (Beliefs):** Your interpretation or belief about the event.
3. **C (Consequences):** Emotional/behavioral reactions caused by beliefs.

---

**Goal:** To swap irrational, self-defeating thoughts for rational, constructive ones.
